Kadai Paneer

Ingredients

2 tbsp coriander seeds
2 tsp cumin
2 tsp fennel
3 dried red chilli
3 dried red chilli
2 tsp pepper
2 tbsp oil
2 tsp ghee
1 tsp cumin
5 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1 inch ginger, finely chopped
2 onion, finely chopped
½ tsp turmeric
1 tsp chilli powder
1 tbsp kadai masala
1 tsp salt
3 tomato, chopped 2 tbsp cream
1 tsp kasuri methi, crushed
2 tbsp coriander, chopped
1 inch ginger, julienne cut
2 tsp oil
onion
capsicum
tomato
paneer
1 tsp garam masala

Heat oil in a kadhai (wok) or a large pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and chopped green chilies. Sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears. Add finely chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and the oil starts to separate. Add tomato puree, red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes until the masala is well cooked. Add sliced green and red bell peppers. Cook for 3-4 minutes until they are slightly tender but still have a crunch. Add paneer cubes and mix gently to coat them with the masala. Add fresh cream and garam masala. Stir well and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes. Add butter for extra richness and flavor. Mix well and cook for a minute. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves. Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice. Enjoy your delicious Kadhai Paneer! Adjust the spice levels according to your preference.
